Welcome to Open Genome Explorer!

Open Genome Explorer is a web app that utilizes the genetic information you upload to provide insights about your genome. It interfaces with open source SNP databases, such as OpenSNP, to display information about your genetic risk factors based on allele combinations.

Get Started

Create an account

Sign up or log in using your Google account to save and access your genomic data at any time

Upload your genomic information

Share your genetic data from sources like 23&me and compare your information to open source databases

Explore your genome

Discover valuable insights and understanding about your health traits and potential risk factors
